Deep Work examples

Introduction : Deep work is focused work where one concentrates on significant amount of time on some intellectual pursuits

We live in a age communication and information overload. We are drowned in the mountain of unwanted information. While all these tools and techniques are supposed to make us more productive the opposite is happening. At the center of this is our inability to understand how the mind works and is structured.

Some of the people who have the deep work practices :

  • Yuval Harari - historian
  • Cal Newport -computer scientist
  • John Grisham - novelist
  • Albert Einstein
